This is a prototype, it's a bit rough around the edges, but functionally, it works.

Some caveats:

  • the skills and competencies are exactly as they are in the DDaT framework
  • the skills and competencies are not tailored to DfE
  • this service won't show what to do to improve skill gaps (see future)
  • you can't edit or delete any updates or comments (yet - see future)

What you can do:

  • see the skills and competencies for your role and grade
  • save how you feel you meet the skills
  • see what skills and competencies are for the next grade in your profession
  • add actions to a plan to track progress on developing any skills

Future:

  • edit and close actions and update comments
  • add other skills outside skills and capability framework
  • compare against others in the same profession anonymously
  • share your results and plan with your line manager or a profession manager
  • reminders for completing and updating actions
  • training plans for skills gaps
  • make it work on Azure Active Directory so no need for magic links

Feedback and ideas

If you have any feedback about this prototype or any ideas for making it better, contact Design Ops.
